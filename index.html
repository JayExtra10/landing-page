Our Story

Built on trust, crafted with precision

Benid Industries was founded with a singular commitment: to manufacture world-class alcoholic beverages that honour Nigerian and West African culture. Rooted in Lagos and operational across the region, we combine modern production technology with time-honoured craft to deliver consistent, premium-quality products to our partners.

Manufacturing Excellence
Our state-of-the-art facility operates under strict quality protocols, ensuring every bottle that leaves our plant meets the highest industry standards.
Pan-West African Presence
From Lagos to Accra, our distribution footprint spans major West African markets, giving our partners unmatched reach and market visibility.
Partner-First Business Model
We prioritise our distributors and wholesale partners with competitive pricing, reliable supply chains, and dedicated account support.
